INTERIOR DESIGN

Our construction experience in London includes adding storeys to Victorian blocks of flats in conservation areas, new builds for private homes, basements in listed buildings and more. We create, make and test ideas in our London studio, solving problems with our in-house human centred design expertise and in partnership with our network of specialists.

PROJECT ANALYSIS

We have written feasibility studies for a diverse range of industries and businesses, including lounge, real estate, product launching, branches and much more.

Interior Design & Branding

Akart Ltd. is an well-established interior architecture practice working with brands, businesses and private clients who share their passion for detail, craft and refinement.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Our project management services cover the whole project, from inception to completion. We treat each project as unique and we maintain momentum, which we see as two of the keys to project success.

Architectural Design

The team consists of Akart Ltd registered architects, structural engineers and experienced architectural designers. Taking a dedicated approach to each project, we meet clients face-to-face to talk through the step-by-step process.
